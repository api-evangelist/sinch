<p>We’re&nbsp;officially stepping into a new phase in&nbsp;both&nbsp;MessageMedia&nbsp;and&nbsp;Sinch’s&nbsp;brand journey.&nbsp;</p>



<p>You may know that Sinch acquired MessageMedia, a global messaging provider, back in 2021. Since then, the MessageMedia platform has been operating as Sinch MessageMedia, helping 10,000+ customers worldwide to send reliable and personalized communications.  But now, MessageMedia is taking it’s next step. </p>



<p>To create a more unified experience for both our MessageMedia and Sinch customers, and to also enable ourselves to innovate faster at a global scale,<strong> MessageMedia is moving closer to the Sinch family by rebranding to Sinch Engage in the US and UK</strong>. </p>


<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-large is-resized"><img alt="" class="wp-image-182937" height="630" src="https://sinch.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/MessageMedia-is-Sinch-Engage-1024x630.png" style="width: 600px;" width="1024" /></figure>
</div>


<p>This change ultimately means that, if you’re a MessageMedia customer in these regions, you get the same easy and reliable messaging experience you love, but soon to be further backed by the power and innovation of the global Sinch brand and ecosystem.  </p>



<p>And if you&#8217;re not a current customer, this means that you’ll begin to see less of the MessageMedia name and more of our new brand identity: Sinch Engage.  </p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>What&#8217;s changing and when?</strong> </h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Starting today, MessageMedia will begin its rebrand to Sinch Engage in the US and UK, but the change is not all happening at once.  </li>
</ul>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The first change is the website: The MessageMedia website will be sunset in the US and UK on April 1. After that, all visitors from those regions will be redirected to its new home: <a href="https://sinch.com/engage/messagemedia/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>sinch.com/engage/messagemedia</strong></a> </li>
</ul>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The rebrand is not yet impacting the MessageMedia app in these regions – we will give plenty of notice when that changes.  </li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>What this means for <strong>US and UK</strong> MessageMedia customers</strong></h2>



<p>Unifying our brands&nbsp;the right way&nbsp;is a&nbsp;process&nbsp;and&nbsp;we’re&nbsp;committed to making this transition as smooth as possible, with&nbsp;minimal&nbsp;disruption&nbsp;to your experience.&nbsp;Here’s&nbsp;what you need to know:&nbsp;</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Your MessageMedia account and app remain the same</strong>. For now, the app is not changing, and your account details and plan are not affected in any way. </li>
</ul>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>MessageMedia login is also unchanged</strong>. The login for all current MessageMedia customers continues to be <a href="https://hub.messagemedia.com/login" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>hub.messagemedia.com</strong></a><strong>.</strong> </li>
</ul>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>You can still use MessageMedia support documents. </strong>We’ve kept our support site intact as well, so you can continue to get help when you need it: <a href="https://support.messagemedia.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>support.messagemedia.com</strong></a>. </li>
</ul>



<p>If you have any questions, our customer support team is&nbsp;always&nbsp;here to help.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>What this means for MessageMedia customers in AU and NZ</strong> </h2>



<p>For&nbsp;MessageMedia&nbsp;customers in AU and NZ, there are&nbsp;<strong>no changes to your&nbsp;brand or app&nbsp;experience&nbsp;at this time</strong>.&nbsp;The&nbsp;website&nbsp;remains&nbsp;the same&nbsp;if you’re visiting from AU or NZ, and&nbsp;your&nbsp;<a href="https://hub.messagemedia.com/login" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">MessageMedia&nbsp;login</a>, account, and plan, remain exactly the same as usual.&nbsp;<br />&nbsp;<br />Later this year,&nbsp;the&nbsp;MessageMedia&nbsp;rebrand&nbsp;will&nbsp;eventually roll out in&nbsp;AU and NZ,&nbsp;but you can rest assured that&nbsp;we will communicate any changes&nbsp;well in advance.&nbsp;Watch this space – and your email inbox &#8211; for&nbsp;updates.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>What’s next?</strong> </h2>



<p>While the&nbsp;MessageMedia&nbsp;name is changing, our mission&nbsp;isn’t. Sinch Engage offers the same easy-to-use, secure messaging solution for businesses that want to build real connections with customers across SMS, MMS, RCS, WhatsApp, and more – all from one hub.&nbsp;</p>



<p>If you still have more questions:&nbsp;</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Reach out to your customer success rep anytime </li>



<li>Or you can always <a href="https://support.messagemedia.com/hc/en-us/articles/10439522859407-Contact-support" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">contact support</a> </li>
</ul>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>FAQ</strong>&nbsp;</h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>What is changing?</strong> <br />MessageMedia is rebranding to Sinch Engage to create a more unified brand experience with its parent company, Sinch. The first change will be the MessageMedia website in the US and UK, which will be sunset on April 1 and redirected to <a href="https://sinch.com/engage/messagemedia/">a new page on the Sinch website</a>.</li>
</ul>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>How does this rebrand impact me if I&#8217;m a MessageMedia customer in the US or UK?</strong> <br />For now, the rebrand&#8217;s impact is minimal and mainly entails the website sunsetting in the US and UK. Your MessageMedia account, the app itself, and your account details remain the same. The rebrand will not yet affect the app, and we will provide plenty of notice before any changes occur. </li>



<li class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>I’m a customer in Australia or New Zealand. What’s changing for me?</strong> <br />There are no changes to your brand or app experience at this time for AU and NZ customers. The rebrand will eventually roll out in these regions, and we will communicate all changes well in advance. </li>



<li class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>Where do I log into MessageMedia account?</strong> <br />Your login process has not changed. All MessageMedia customers can continue to log in at <a href="https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fhub.messagemedia.com%2Flogin" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">hub.messagemedia.com</a>. </li>



<li class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>How do I contact support?</strong> <br />You can continue to use the MessageMedia support site to get help at <a href="https://support.messagemedia.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">support.messagemedia.com</a>. If you have questions, you can also reach out to your customer success representative, or <a href="https://support.messagemedia.com/hc/en-us/articles/10439522859407-Contact-support" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">contact support</a>. </li>



<li class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>Will this impact my pricing and billing?</strong>&nbsp;<br />No, the website sunset does not&nbsp;impact&nbsp;your plan pricing or account billing.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
