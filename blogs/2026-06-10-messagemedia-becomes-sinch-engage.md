Starting 28 July 2026, Sinch MessageMedia officially rebrands to Sinch Engage. MessageMedia has been helping businesses connect with their customers for over 20 years. In 2021, Sinch acquired MessageMedia – bringing together trusted messaging expertise and a global communications network under one roof.
