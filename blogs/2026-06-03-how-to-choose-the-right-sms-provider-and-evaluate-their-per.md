When selecting an SMS provider, businesses must evaluate critical factors including integration ease, security compliance, and delivery performance. According to Sinch research, SMS remains one of the most widely used communication channels across industries, supporting everything from order confirmations to fraud alerts. Choosing the right SMS provider is a strategic business decision, not merely a technical one.
